Welcome to Mr. Noodleman!

So, uhh, what IS this?


Mr. Noodleman is the story of a severed hand that gained sentience.

It follows the eponymous finger through an allegorical journey through the
world of the Fish Head. His life, from its very beginning, is a surreal adventure.
Join him in his travels, and be thoroughly confused.


Webcomic updated irregularly, around once a month
