About Mr. Noodleman


About the storyline

Mr. Noodleman is a surrealist allegory. It's not supposed to entirely make sense. There is little logic to the world, the characters don't do what you would expect, the premise in general is nonsensical. There's a deeper meaning. Don't question my strangeness.

About the art

Mr. Noodleman is written, penciled and inked by Ethan Shilo-Draper. All pages are originally ink on paper, with some digital manipulation for optimal viewing.
